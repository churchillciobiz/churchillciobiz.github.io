Mobilet Dashboard Features

Inventory & branch Manager

Be it clothing, restaurant, gift shop whatever nature of store. Mobilet terminal has the right solution for you to manage, showcase and sell your items on the mobilet app. Add, remove, update items to your Mobilet Mini app store, Create Store branches, Create item categories, add items to those categories, add items to those individual Store branches all through the Mobilet dashboard

Dashboard Analytics

Get the facts on which items in your inventory are trending for the day, Analyse trends on item sales, branch performance, category performance etc

Map Analytics

Pinpoint where users are interacting with the app eg through comments,purchases thus equipping your with the power to geographically strategise your business, if your branch locations are in the system see how much traffic each is generating through heat maps

Consumer Feedback

Manage orders, get valueable incites from users through comments posted on your Mini App comment section and review your activities in the Mobilet Dashoard all in one clean clear interface

Mobilet App Features

Mini App

Your own personal mini apps that runs within the Mobilet app environment, highly customizable for showcasing and informing or make it a full ecommerce mini app to sell items to consumers its also adaptable to show items present in one branch from items present in another branch

Run Promotions

Create and run promotions on the Mobilet app that target consumers based on their buying trends for example where they buy and how much they spend on average

Your shop in qr

One scan of a QR code near or at your physical store gives the potential customer access to a digital version of your physical shop and items in the shop, not only is your shop scannable but items too can be scanned by the Mobilet app user who can to get information documented by you on the item

Easy to find you

Leverage on "Mobilet Augment" to enable your physical shop to be noticed upto 10km away by prospective consumers we are working on advancing the capability of the system to enable consumers filter the shops the want(eg cothing, restaurant, events etc) or rating of service
